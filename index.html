Admission Process & Eligibility

Steps for Applying

  1. Choose your preferred country and university.
  2. Submit required documents including transcripts and passport copies.
  3. Receive your admission letter and pay the initial fees.
  4. Apply for a student visa and prepare for your journey.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Minimum 50% in 12th grade with Physics, Chemistry, and Biology.
  • NEET qualification mandatory for Indian students.
  • Must be 17 years or older by the time of admission.
